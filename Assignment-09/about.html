Why Did I Choose Direct Input Recording?

I chose to do an instructable on the method of digital audio recording because I thought it would be relatively simple to explain. It is also something that I am good at, so I felt confident in my ability to properly explain the step-by-step process. I could have gone much more in-depth on the settings within the digital audio workstation or within the audio interface, but I tried to focus only on the fundamental setup in order to keep it simple.
