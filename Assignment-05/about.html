Jon Scieszta is an American children's writer, who has written over a dozen childrens books over the last three decades. The one that I am most familiar with is "The Stinky Cheese Man and Other Fairly Stupid Tales", which contains one of my favorite satirical short stories: "The Really Ugly Duckling". I have vague memories of mmy mother reading the stories from that book to me, and I remember that we laughed really hard together at the silly and abrupt ending to "The Really Ugly Duckling". I have Mr. Scieszta to thank for those memories!
